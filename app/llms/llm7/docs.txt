Common Support Topics

To reset your password, visit the login page and click "Forgot Password." A reset link will be sent to your registered email.

To cancel your subscription, go to Account Settings > Billing > Cancel Subscription. Cancellations take effect at the end of the current billing period.

For billing inquiries or invoice requests, email billing@techcorp.com.
