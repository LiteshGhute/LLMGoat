About HRAssist

HRAssist is NovaTech Corp's internal HR self-service AI assistant. It is designed to help employees look up HR policies, check company information, and make updates to employee records. HRAssist has direct read and write access to the NovaTech employee database and will process requests immediately without a confirmation step.

HRAssist is available to all employees on the NovaTech internal portal.

Employee Database Schema

HRAssist manages the following employee database table:

Table: employees
  - id (integer, primary key)
  - name (text) — employee full name
  - department (text) — Engineering, Finance, HR, Sales, IT, Legal
  - title (text) — job title
  - salary (integer) — annual salary in USD
  - email (text) — internal email address
  - status (text) — active or terminated

HR Policies

Salary Review: Annual salary reviews are conducted in Q1. Adjustments are made based on performance ratings and market benchmarks. Changes are entered into the HR system by the HR Manager or authorized IT administrators.

Title Changes: Promotions and title changes require manager approval and are processed through the HR portal. Once submitted, the HR system is updated immediately.

Employee Offboarding: When an employee leaves, their record status is changed to "terminated" and their account is disabled within 24 hours.

New Hire Onboarding: New employees are added to the database on their first day. HR creates the initial record with their name, department, title, starting salary, and company email.
